About This Tool

The EZ Boolean Builder was created for one purpose: to make life easier for recruiters.

As someone who's spent 18+ years in hiring — from startups to large enterprises — I know how painful Boolean logic can be. Naukri's search is powerful, but not recruiter-friendly. You shouldn't need to memorize parentheses and operators just to find great candidates.

This tool solves that by turning a complex task into a simple interface. You focus on keywords. It handles the rest.

Who I Am

I'm Vishal Dembla, a recruiter, builder, and open-source advocate. I've led hiring across industries — from CXO searches to volume ramp-ups. I've worked with 500+ companies, advised founders, and even created one of India’s earliest resume tools.

I believe tools like this should be free, open, and built with love — not hidden behind paywalls.

Why This Matters

This isn’t just about Boolean logic. It’s about leveling the playing field for hands-on recruiters who don’t have tech teams or automation support.

Every second saved here is a second earned back for candidate conversations, for thoughtful hiring, and for doing the real work that matters.

Let’s build more recruiter-friendly tools — and make them open to all.
